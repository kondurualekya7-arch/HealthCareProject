# Power BI Storyboard (Hospital Operations View)

## Must-have visuals
1. KPI cards
   - No-show rate
   - High-risk appointment count
   - Reschedule recommendation count
2. No-shows by demographics
   - Age group, gender
3. No-shows by operations
   - Weekday, appointment period (morning/afternoon)
4. Communication effectiveness
   - SMS sent vs no-show rate
   - Number of reminders vs no-show rate
5. Heatmap
   - Weekday vs appointment period with no-show rate
6. Trend view
   - No-show rate over appointment date

## Slicers
- Date range
- Neighbourhood (clinic proxy)
- Booking channel
- Risk band
- Sentiment label

## Story flow
- Start with trend and KPI impact
- Drill into risk drivers (lead time, reminders, transport)
- End with operational action queue and schedule recommendations
